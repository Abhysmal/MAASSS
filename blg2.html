Style Unplugged

Our mission at MAASSS is to support your individuality with cutting-edge, adaptable, and distinctly you clothing. With this guide, we're tackling the newest styles in apparel and accessories in an offline manner. This guide can help you discover that statement piece or give your wardrobe a makeover.

Renaissance of Athleisure
Although comfort is important, why sacrifice style? There is still a tendency in athleisure to conflate high design with casual clothes. MAASSS provides fashionable and functional fitted sweatshirts, athletic hoodies, and elegant joggers. For the ultimate athleisure style, pair them with statement accessories like giant tote bags or clunky trainers.

Strong Prints
From geometric designs to animal prints, bold prints are really popular right now. At MAASSS, we welcome this trend with eye-catching accessories that give every ensemble flair. If you're not ready to commit to a whole print, consider accessorizing with patterned scarves or brightly coloured shoes to quickly uplift your look.

Bold Jewelry
When it comes to accessories, more really is better. Oversized bracelets, big hoop earrings, and chunky necklaces are trendy right now. To locate the ideal item, browse our collection of statement jewelry at MAASSS.
